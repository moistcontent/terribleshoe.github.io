About Me

I like aviation, minimal programming, and I have an issue when things aren't placed like they should be. I'm passionate about a few things, I'm pretty selfish when it comes to those things and I'm also stubborn as hell. But who cares right? It is what it is.

Aviation

My love for aviation startted as a young child, it's all I've ever wanted to do. There's just something so spectacular to watch behind the creation and the theory of a flight. It's one of the best things I'm still optimistic about. My current plan is to get my commercial pilot's license (CPL) from the FAA, come back to India and convert it to a DGCA CPL and fly with a regional airline, like SpiceJet or IndiGo.

Music

I'm really into music, I can't play any instruments but I love the bass. Some of my favourite artists are Radiohead, Red Hot Chili Peppers, Mac DeMarco, The Backseat Lovers, The Black Keys, The Bluestones, The Comet is Coming, and Beach House. I mainly listen to alternative rock, or experimental/math rock. Do send me an email if you've got music you'd think I enjoy.
